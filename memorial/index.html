Portrait of Guo Moruo

Mr. Guo Moruo was indeed a remarkable figure in Chinese history—a visionary scholar, revolutionary poet, and pioneering educator. As the founding president of the University of Science and Technology of China, he established a legacy of academic excellence and innovation. USTC stands today as a cradle of scientific discovery, nurturing brilliant minds who continue to push the boundaries of knowledge. His profound contributions to literature, history, and science remain an enduring inspiration, illuminating the path for future generations of scholars and thinkers...
